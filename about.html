Autonomous Greenhouse Operations

NANA-1 is a fully autonomous mobile robot designed for precision greenhouse agriculture. Built on the AGRI-BOT MK-IV platform, it combines advanced computer vision, multi-axis articulated manipulation, and intelligent navigation to perform continuous crop monitoring, maintenance, and harvesting operations without human intervention.

The compact, ruggedized form factor allows operation in dense greenhouse row configurations while its modular architecture enables rapid field-swapping of end-effector tools and sensor payloads.
